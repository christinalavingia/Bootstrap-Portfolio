About Me

My name is Christina Lavingia. I have a background in content production and am working to broaden my skillset to encompass programming. My goal is to be able to write, wireframe and code webpages from start to finish.

When I'm not working (or studying) I love to read, win at Fantasy Football, eat tacos, watch sunsets and wine taste. Basically many of the staples of living in Santa Barbara, California.

Despite being brand new to web dev, one of my most proud career accomplishments has to do with launching a new website for my current employer.

We went from one static page that briefly touched on some of our features, to a dozen pages each diving in detail to better sell our product.

We're looking forward to the SEO benefits of having more pages, as well as further lead gen opportunities with additional CTAs — now on every page.

I hope that this program through UCLA helps me pursue a richer career path in marketing and brand management. It's a unique path to be taking, but so far I'm loving making pages just like these.
